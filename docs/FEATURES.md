# Features

Everything Zwäg can do, in one place. All personal data stays on the device: there is no account, no server and no tracking.

## Diary

![Diary](screenshots/diary.jpg)

- Daily summary card with a remaining calorie ring, eaten and burned stats and carb, protein and fat bars
- Four meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks) with per meal calorie budgets, progress rings and a preview of the logged foods
- Water card with a tappable glass grid and a daily goal
- Weight quick log that updates the calorie target and writes to Apple Health
- Steps card with a 7 day mini chart from Apple Health
- Mood card to capture how the day felt
- Streak flame with milestone celebrations (3, 7, 14, 30, 50, 100, 200, 365 days) and confetti
- Calendar to browse any past day, plus a day detail page with balance, macro targets and a body fat estimate
- "Like yesterday" shortcut copies yesterday's meal into an empty meal

## Buddy

![Buddy studio](screenshots/studio.jpg)

- A personal mascot that lives in the diary header and reacts to your day: sleeping when nothing is logged, happy on track, sweating over budget, party at milestones
- Three ways to get one: dice roll from 500+ bundled characters, the Avatar Studio wardrobe with live preview, or your own photo
- Body buddy on the progress page whose belly follows your BMI and slims down as you lose weight

## Recipes

![Recipes](screenshots/recipes.jpg) 

- 890 recipes: Swiss classics and everyday dishes plus 17 international cuisines from Italian to Korean
- Browse by category, calories, diet or cuisine
- Every recipe logs into the diary with one tap, portions adjustable
- Shopping list built from recipe ingredients
- Recipe photos are community contributed: every recipe page links to the website where anyone can submit their photo

## Scanner

![Scanner](screenshots/scanner.jpg)

- Live EAN barcode scanning with product lookup on Open Food Facts
- Scanned products are cached on device: repeat scans are instant and work offline
- Label mode: photograph the nutrition table of any product and on device text recognition reads the per 100 g values into a new custom food (German, English, French and Italian labels)
- The official Swiss Food Composition Database (BLV, 1220 foods) is bundled for offline search
- Custom foods with per 100 g values, reusable in search and scanner, with optional barcode
- Sugar, salt and fiber tracked from Open Food Facts where available

## Fasting

![Fasting](screenshots/fasting.jpg)

- Intermittent fasting tracker with 16:8, 14:10 and 12:12 plans
- Live countdown ring, end of fast notification and fasting history
- Live Activity on the lock screen and Dynamic Island while a fast runs

## Battles

![Battles](screenshots/battles.jpg) ![Challenges](screenshots/challenges.jpg)

- Challenge friends over steps, active calories or calorie deficit
- Six character join codes, live leaderboard, medals for the podium
- Scores sync over CloudKit's public database, linked only by the join code. Your own score is computed from the diary and Apple Health and stays up to date even without iCloud; only comparing it with friends needs an account

## Calculators

- BMI with category scale
- Ideal weight
- Daily calorie needs (Mifflin-St Jeor, activity level and goal aware)
- Calorie burn for 50+ activities (MET based)

## Progress

![Progress](screenshots/progress.jpg)

- Weight trend chart (7 days, 30 days, all time)
- Weekly calorie bars and monthly stats
- Week balance card: days on target, weekly calorie balance, water and protein averages
- Body buddy that visualizes your progress

## Apple integration

- HealthKit: reads steps and active energy, writes logged weight
- Home screen widget with the calorie ring, eaten, burned and water
- Live Activity with the day's balance
- Apple Watch app: ring, remaining calories, fasting countdown and one tap water logging
- Watch complications (circular, corner, rectangular and inline)
- Siri Shortcuts: log water and ask for remaining calories

## Personalization

- 23 languages, including Swiss German, Romansh, Albanian, Serbian, Croatian, Bosnian, Turkish, Ukrainian, Tamil, Arabic, Farsi and Tigrinya, with full right to left support
- Three looks: Munch (warm cream), Midnight (dark) and Mono (grayscale), each with a matching app icon
- Custom accent color that restyles the whole app
- Fasting, water goal and reminder times all configurable

## Privacy

- Local first: all personal data stays in the app's database on your device
- No account, no analytics, no ads
- Network is only used for barcode lookups on Open Food Facts and the optional avatar studio preview
