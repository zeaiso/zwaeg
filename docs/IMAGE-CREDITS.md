# Image credits

Recipe photos in Zwäg are community contributed. Every recipe page in the app
and on the website invites users to submit their own photo of the dish.

Published photos are listed here and credited in the app on the recipe page.
The source of truth is `Zwaeg/Resources/RecipeImages/credits.json`.
